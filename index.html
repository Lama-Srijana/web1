Our Speciality

Tibetan Thangka

Tibetan style Thanka is a classical and stylised form of Nepalese and Tibetan painting, consisting of a painted picture panel surrounded by a textile mantle that is supported by scroll sticks and overlain with a silk cover. It is more natural in looks and traditional in appearance of buddhist philosophy.

Newari Thangka

Newari thanka is the painting that present and promote the newari style of art which is rich in design and full of well-define minimalistics works.

Mandala Thanka

mandala is a artistic representation of higher thought and deeper meaning given as a geometric symbol used in spritual, emotional or psychological work to focus one's attention.

Modern Thanka

Modern Thangka is the painting with modern touch which is also called 21st century art. It is the art where painter gives ancient thangka art a contemporary look.

Chinese Thanka

chinese thanka is a painting which present in chinese version.
